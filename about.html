Restoring the
Desktop Experience.

EnhancedTube was born from a simple frustration: watching YouTube on a desktop shouldn't feel like browsing a website. It should feel like an app.

The Mission

Web browsers are great for browsing, but they are heavy, cluttered, and distracting when you just want to focus on content.

EnhancedTube bridges the gap. We take the YouTube experience and wrap it in a high-performance, distraction-free shell. No banner ads, no tracking scripts, just the video and you.
